360° View of Chandigarh Capitol Complex

Historical Background

The Chandigarh Capitol Complex, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a masterpiece of modern architecture designed by Le Corbusier. Located in Chandigarh, India, it houses the Legislative Assembly, Secretariat, and High Court. The Open Hand Monument, a symbol of peace and reconciliation, is a prominent feature of the complex. It represents the spirit of Chandigarh as a city of modernity and progress.

Visitor Rules

  • Maintain silence and respect the sanctity of the site.
  • Photography is allowed but avoid using flash near delicate structures.
  • Do not touch or damage the sculptures and monuments.
  • Follow the instructions of the site staff.

Safety Precautions

  • Wear comfortable footwear as the site involves walking.
  • Carry water to stay hydrated during your visit.
  • Avoid visiting during peak hours to avoid crowds.
