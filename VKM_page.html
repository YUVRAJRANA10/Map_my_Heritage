Historical Background

The Virasat-e-Khalsa Museum, located in Anandpur Sahib, Punjab, is a tribute to the rich history and culture of Sikhism. Opened in 2011, the museum showcases the journey of Sikhism, its ten Gurus, and the values they upheld. The museum's architecture is a blend of modern and traditional styles, designed by Moshe Safdie. It is a must-visit destination for those seeking to understand the spiritual and cultural heritage of Punjab.

Visitor Rules

  • Maintain silence inside the museum to respect its sanctity.
  • Photography is allowed only in designated areas.
  • Do not touch or damage the exhibits.
  • Follow the instructions of the museum staff.

Safety Precautions

  • Wear comfortable shoes as the museum involves walking.
  • Carry water to stay hydrated during your visit.
  • Avoid carrying large bags as they may not be allowed inside.
