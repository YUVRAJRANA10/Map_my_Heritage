360° View of Nalanda University

Historical Background

Nalanda University, a Heritage Site, is located in Bihar, India. Established in the 5th century CE, it was one of the world's first residential universities and a renowned center of learning. The ruins of Nalanda showcase its architectural brilliance and its role as a hub of Buddhist education and culture.

Visitor Rules

  • Maintain silence and respect the sanctity of the site.
  • Photography is allowed but avoid using flash near delicate structures.
  • Do not touch or damage the ruins and sculptures.
  • Follow the instructions of the site staff.

Safety Precautions

  • Wear comfortable footwear as the site involves walking.
  • Carry water to stay hydrated during your visit.
  • Avoid visiting during peak hours to avoid crowds.
