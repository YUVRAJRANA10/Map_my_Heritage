Hawa Mahal - The Pink City's Crown

"The Hawa Mahal is a masterpiece of Rajput architecture. The honeycomb structure and the 953 windows are simply breathtaking. Best time to visit is early morning when the sun's rays create a magical glow on the pink facade."

Visited February 2024
