About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is one of the most iconic monuments in the world. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is a symbol of eternal love. The white marble mausoleum is a masterpiece of Mughal architecture and attracts millions of visitors every year.
